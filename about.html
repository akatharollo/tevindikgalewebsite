About Tevin

GET TO KNOW TEVIN

I am obsessed with emerging businessses that have a potential to thrive in South Africa and I want to get it out there to the world.

It all began in 2010. I was 14 years old in grade 8 and my father had just bought a laptop. Little did they know that I was burning with curiosity. I remember the excitement I had when I first connected to the internet. I went to the shops to buy an airtime voucher of R5, and when I came back I used a dial up connection with a cellphone to connect the laptop to the internet. I was only able to land on the google search page, until the airtime ran out (Laughable I know) but I remember the excitement I had in that moment. It's as if I knew the possibilities that would come with the internet - deep down I knew - that I wanted to work in this exciting online world.

I never stopped asking questions like "How does this work", "What if", "How Might I". This was cemented in me when I was doing my first year. Way before we could cover the HTML & CSS syllabus in class, I had a curiosity of how websites worked. So I went on Udemy and enrolled in a web development course during the first semester. When the second semester came along and we were given a task to make a webpage, it came as an easy task for me compared to my groupmates. That is when I realized the importance of self-research - that I learn better with practical tasks. As a result, most of my hard skills are a product of going through that research, these includes: Website development, Cloud Computing, React Native, UI/UX Design, Graphic Design, Data Analysis and Visualization.

Now, I am passionate about showing new business owners just how easy it is to get their business online and show them the immense joy and freedom the online world gives them! I see South Africa and the whole of the African continent as an utopia of future multi-million/billion businesses and tech as being the driving force to make this potential a success. So my biggest WHY? Is to use the skills that I have acquired to create platforms that will help creatives, bunisess owners, youth to get their ideas and products out there.

My super power is figuring things out which is what my clients love the most about me!
